# Books I read in 2018
<div class="main-content-blog"><p>In 2018, I read 170 books. I gave these books an average rating of 3,58 out of 5. On average, I read 14,17 books a month and 3,27 books a week. Most books were read in June (20). I mostly read psychology books (17), followed by business (15), science (14), and general fiction (14). 26% of books I read were written by female authors.</p></div>
