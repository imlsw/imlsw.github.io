# Books I read in 2019
<div class="main-content-blog"><p>In 2019, I read 75 books. I gave these books an average rating of 3,84 out of 5. On average, I read 6,25 books a month and 1,44 books a week. Most books were read in March (13). I mostly read self help books (9), followed by business (8) and general fiction (8). 40% of books I read were written by female authors.</p></div>
