# Books I read in 2017
<div class="main-content-blog"><p>In 2017, I read 55 books. I gave these books an average rating of 3,74 out of 5. On average, I read 4,58 books a month and 1,06 books a week. Most books were read in April (12). I mostly read self help books (9), followed by business (7), and history (5). 25% of books I read were written by female authors.</p></div>
